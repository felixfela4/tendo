Introduction

our story

The beginning...

The desire for serving God, supporting the Church, helping vulnerable and needy children, women and families of Samuel and Olivia Kiggundu led to the birth of Tendo La Upendo Uganda (TLU) which means ( Act of Love) with an intent of seeking for individuals and organisations with which to partner in this Nobel Couse. We started in 2012 with sponsoring 20 needy children in kituntu subcounty,Mpigi district-central Uganda and empowering pastors and church leaders through Conferences and seminars.

Tendo La Upendo Uganda is a tool for preaching the word of our Lord Jesus Christ in our communities, reaching out to the orphans, needy children and women aiming at giving them hope and a future. In the future we hope to develop fully sustainable projects that will raise funds locally to provide for all the projects of Tendo La Upendo Uganda . .However, this goal is still some years away.
