About Us

who we are,why we exist.

Tendo La Upendo Uganda a Christian faith based non-profit making organization dedicated to empowering children, families, church leaders and communities to reach their God given potential to live life in all its fullness.

we exist to :

  • To preach the gospel of our Lord, Jesus Christ as the only way through which man must be reconciled to God from his sinful nature, through Salvation by faith.
  • To help those who become Christians through receiving the Lord Jesus as Saviour and Lord to grow toward Spiritual maturity and understanding and to train them to reach others with the Gospel. The objects being fulfilment of the Great Commission of our Lord Jesus Christ.
  • To provide love and care to People Living with HIV/ AIDS (PHA), and being and show them God’s Love practically.
  • To promote Christian educational welfare of children in Uganda and open up Christian Schools, Colleges, Centers, Institutes, Bible Colleges and Day-care centers
  • To co-operate with any churches, missions, organizations in activities and programs designed to bring about peace in the countries
  • To set up needy children support programs, Orphanages Women and Youth Development Projects, Financial Empowerment Programs, Children Sponsorships and establish training centres
  • To establish Self-help projects in areas of vocational skills, Agriculture and Small-scale Industries and/ or to engage in Agricultural projects
  • To purchase or take on lease by any means acquire any movable or immovable property in Uganda or elsewhere for the work of promoting the Ministry’s objects
  • To employ with or without remuneration such officers, servants, and other persons as the Ministry may think fit, for the carrying out of its objects
  • To do all other things as are incidental or necessary to the attainment of the above objects or any of them.
